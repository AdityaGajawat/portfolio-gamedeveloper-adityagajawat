    • DEVELOPMENT

    Slash is a Melee-Action prototype. I was inspired from a prototype available on itch.io which is a Multiplayer action-combat between 2 Samurai using Katana to attack and pushing opponent to defend. After experiencing refreshing gameplay I had an idea of mixing concept of Melee+Defence with RPG elements.

  • Selecting Assets
    • Used Unity's Asset Store & itch.io Asset Store to import best suitable asset.

  • Player Behaviour
    • Setting up player with testable Movement & Behaviour mechanics and Animations. Using improved C# coding methods, animator setup & more.

  • Enemy & A.I.
    • There are 2 types of enemies with different approach on Behaviours.

  • Colliders
    • Attached several colliders to Player & Enemy object to detect different interactions, ground detection, opponent overlap detection, in-range detection are few of them.

    Didn't followed Object Oriented Approach for this project. Now that I think about it, would be better to do so. Tried my best to showcase my idea & I will be continuing with the development.



    Parkour Runner 2D is an Endless Runner in which player has to tackle obtacles performing parkour moves and break highest score!

  • Selecting Assets
    • Assets were provided by the instructor of the course.

  • Player Behaviour
    • Didn't blindly followed the instructor instead tried achieving similar results by my own coding methods. It was little tricky for me to setup Player Controller + Animations, state machines for transition between animation on basis of variables. Setting up different colliders for wall detection, ground detection and ledge detection was fun to implement.

  • Obstacles
    • Colliding with obstacles is game-over but with good momentum it can be avoided!

    Currently working on mobile version of the game!
